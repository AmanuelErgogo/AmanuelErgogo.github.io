Hi, I’m Amanuel.

I’m a Ph.D. student in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of South Florida, advised by Prof. Zhao Han. I have a background in automation and robotics, with over two years of experience in assistive AI robotics for healthcare at the Sano Center for Computational Medicine.

Projects

HiLoTEL

Virtual-reality framework for learning and executing tasks in virtual and physical environments. Human experts collaborate with learning agents and intervene when needed (human-in-the-loop).

  • Reduces repetitive task execution by enabling intuitive supervision.
  • Unity3D digital twin on a VR headset; ROS for VR-to-robot communication.

Robot learning from demonstration + Deep RL (Robot-assisted feeding)

Contact-rich food scooping manipulation. Human experts demonstrate scooping with a 3D space mouse, then a policy is trained on demonstrations and fine-tuned in a reinforcement learning environment.

Pick novel objects with Deep RL (Sim-to-Real)

  • Contact-GraspNet plans grasps for unknown geometry/mechanics.
  • Trained on synthetic data from ~17M trials in simulation.
  • Tested on 7-DOF arm with unseen objects (~80% success).

Model-based control of a two-wheeled self-balancing robot

  • Controller built from a physical model (Simscape Multibody) in Simulink.
  • Model-based control law allows self-balancing.

Wind Turbine Generator

Main objective: Build a low-cost turbine to generate electricity from wind.

  • Generated ~5V DC with PMDC generators.
  • Used to power compound lights at family home.
  • Challenge: regulation due to air-gap flux behavior.

Cable Insulation Eccentricity Measuring & Correcting System

Objective: Measure/correct insulation thickness errors during extrusion.

  • Designed sensor + signal conditioning system.
  • Simulated correction due to lack of hardware interface.
  • Tech: Atmega MCU, capacitive depth sensor, stepper motor.

Projector-Scarcity Solution (Lab Projector App)

Built an app to act as a projector for labs, enabling real-time control of questions/answers/instructions and anonymous student feedback.
